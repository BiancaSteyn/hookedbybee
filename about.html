About Hooked by Bee 🐝💜

More About Me

Hi there! I'm Bee — a young entrepreneur with a passion for handmade creations and cozy comforts. Crochet started as a hobby and quickly grew into something I love sharing with others. In my free time, I enjoy crocheting, reading good books, and when inspiration strikes — writing a little myself! I’ve even published a book, and now I’m simply exploring where life takes me.

When I’m not crocheting, you can usually find me teaching as a full-time swimming instructor, which doesn’t leave me with much free time — but every stitch I make is filled with love and dedication. I’m also currently studying law, balancing my creative and academic passions one day at a time. 💜

Need More Information?

When placing an order, I always try to respond as soon as possible. Sometimes I might be in the pool (literally 🏊‍♀️), so please be patient if I can’t reply immediately — I’ll get back to you as soon as I climb out!

Because of my busy schedule, creating each item might take a little extra time, but I promise to dedicate every spare moment to completing your order as soon as possible.

For larger or multiple-item orders, I do require a **deposit** to cover yarn and materials. Full payment is required before delivery. If a third-party delivery service is needed, customers are responsible for **80% of the delivery cost**. Thank you for your understanding and support — every order means the world to me! 🐝✨

Materials Used

I use a variety of yarns depending on the project — from soft cotton and premium acrylic to chunky textured blends. You can even **customize your yarn type or color** when placing your order!

For amigurumi and stuffed animals, I use safety-pegged eyes, durable stuffing, and sometimes a bit of flexible wire for structure (depending on the design). Everything is assembled carefully to ensure your handmade piece is both adorable and long-lasting. 🧶

Source Supplies

Most of my yarn and crochet supplies come from Habby Hyper — my go-to store for quality materials! Occasionally, I also purchase from Temu, Shein, and ValueCo when I need something unique or hard to find locally.
